About

My Story

Welcome to my portfolio! I'm a multi-passionate UX/UI Designer with a background in financial operations and management. I've spent most of my career at the intersection of finance and tech, conducting research and testing, and collaborating with developers to understand the user experience and help streamline operational processes. After working on these projects, I was inspired to pursue UX design. In addition to my work experience, I've studied UI Design at Parsons School of Design and completed a UX Immersion course through CareerFoundry. When I am not designing, I love to travel, run, figure skate and spend time with friends and family. I am also Vice Chair of the Off Broadway Theatre, an active alumna of the Delta Alpha Sigma Multicultural Sorority, and an Academic Coach at the University of Texas at Arlington.
